Underwater GPS - for divers!

Today we are launching a brand new product which is perferctly suited for divers! We call it the "Locator-U1" and it has these special features:

- Battery powered
- Fully wireless, no cable requirements

The Locator-U1 is therefore perfect for divers or underwater vehicles with limited integration possibilities. We expect this product to find its way into many different dive applications like dive boats, dive clubs, dive resorts but also ROVs with limitied possibilities to integrate cabled products.

The product is made available for pre-order today with the first orders shipping in March 2018. Make sure to pre-order to secure a special pre-order discount!
